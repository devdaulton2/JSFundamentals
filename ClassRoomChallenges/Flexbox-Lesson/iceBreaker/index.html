Talk to the hand

Instructions

You are going to create an html unorganized list to describe things about you based on the hand.

  • Pointer Finger: Someone you look up to
  • Middle Finger: Something that really bothers you
  • Ring Finger: Something you love to do
  • Pinky Finger: Something you could be better at
  • Thumb: Thumbthing you would be doing right now if you were not in class
